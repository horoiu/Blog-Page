About Me

Hi. My name is Marian Horoiu but all my friends are calling me Mario.

I was born in Galati in the early 80's. I finished the engineering college in early 2000's, got married and relocated in Braila, where I happily lived with my family till this year.

After 15 years I have decided to make a huge change in my carrer, therefore I quit my job and now I'm looking to relocate with my family to Cluj-Napoca, in order to fulfill my dream of teenage, and that is to became a programmer/developer.

Background

All my working career, which is 15 years by the way, is based on offshore drilling rigs. For the last 10 years I was a Barge Captain/Rig Mover and some of my main responsibilities were:

  • weekly train and test all personnel onboard on emergency situations response (fire, man overboard, poisonous gases, abandon ship, medical evacuations ...);
  • prepare and move the offshore rig from one location to another one, once the drilling operations were completed;
  • ensure that I have everything onboard in time so the drilling process will not be interrupted (a rig daily rate is >100k USD).

My highest achievement in the offshore career was in 2015 when I was detached in Singapore's company office as a Project Barge Engineer and I was participating in the construction and commissioning of the new-build jack-up rig "ARABDRILL-70" in Keppel-Fels shipyard, and the dry transportation of the new rig from Singapore to Bahrain.

My projects

CNP check

This is my 1st JavaScript project. I mean the 1st important JavaScript project, besides the "Hello World!" .... and "Click Me!" ....

You can follow the link in the title and check it out.

Briefly, what I did there is to ask from the user to type in an CNP, Birth Year, Birth Month and Birth Day.

By clincking the "Verify CNP" button the JavaScript will check if:

  1. is there any field that is left empty;
  2. if CNP is starting with "0"; if is a valid number and if its lenght is of 13 characters;
  3. if Birth Year is a valid number; if its lenght is 4 characters it will check if is smaller than 1799 or greather than 2018 then will extract and use only the last 2 digits;
  4. if Birth Month is a valid number and between 1 and 12; if its length is 1 character only, it will add "0" in front of it;
  5. if Birth Day is a valid number and between 1 and 31; if its length is 1 character only, it will add "0" in front of it;
  6. after all input data is processed, it will check if the Birth date is corresponding to data from CNP;
  7. and best of all, it will tell you if the CNP you entered is a real one or not.
